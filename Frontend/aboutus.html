Our Story

Discover the passion and dedication behind CS Antiques, where history meets craftsmanship

Objects with a past, pieces for your future

CS Antiques is a home for storied originals, especially Pettagam chests, the traditional wooden trunks loved for their honest craftsmanship and timeless character. We handpick each piece for quality, proportion, and patina, then restore it with respect so it's ready for modern living.

From elegant Pettagam chests to curated furniture and decor, our collection is chosen for lasting beauty, not trends. Every piece comes with clear details, thoughtful care, and styling guidance if you need it.

Bring home a little history that's made to be used and loved. Our team of expert restorers and antique enthusiasts work tirelessly to preserve the integrity of each piece while ensuring it fits seamlessly into contemporary homes.

Our Values

Preservation

We believe in preserving the integrity and history of each antique, maintaining its original character while ensuring it remains functional for modern use.

Authenticity

Every piece in our collection is thoroughly vetted for authenticity. We provide complete provenance and historical context for significant items.

Craftsmanship

We celebrate the skilled artisanship of the past and present, supporting traditional crafts and restoration techniques that honor original work.
