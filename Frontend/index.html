CS Antiques

CS Antiques is a home for storied originals, especially Pettagam chests, the traditional wooden trunks loved for their honest craftsmanship and timeless character. We handpick each piece for quality, proportion, and patina, then restore it with respect so it's ready for modern living. From elegant Pettagam chests to curated furniture and decor, our collection is chosen for lasting beauty, not trends. Every piece comes with clear details, thoughtful care, and styling guidance if you need it. Bring home a little history that's made to be used and loved.
