This exquisite Vintage Pettagam Chest is a remarkable example of traditional Sri Lankan craftsmanship from the early 20th century. Pettagam chests were traditionally used to store valuable items, clothing, and important documents in Sri Lankan households.

This particular piece has been carefully restored by our expert craftsmen to preserve its historical integrity while ensuring it remains functional for modern use. The chest features intricate carvings of traditional motifs, original brass hardware, and a rich patina that can only develop over decades of careful use.

The interior includes a removable tray with compartments, perfect for organizing smaller items. The wood has been treated with natural oils to enhance its beauty and provide protection, while the metal fittings have been gently polished to restore their luster without diminishing their antique character.
